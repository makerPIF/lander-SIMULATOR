⑤ Entry, Descent & Landing (EDL) phases
1
Aeroshell / heatshield entry
Hypersonic drag + 1,500°C heating. Entry speed: 5–20 km/s → ~1.5 km/s. Used on: all planets with atmosphere.
2
Parachute deployment if atmosphere
Subsonic/supersonic drag. 1.5 km/s → ~80-300 km/h. Skipped on Moon & Mercury. Very short on Venus.
3
Rocket retropropulsion Moon/Mars critical
GNC takes full control. Final 2 km → 0 m/s. Uses IMU + radar altimeter + computer. This is the GNC simulator's focus.
4
Landing gear impact absorption
Final 1–2 m. Crushable legs, springs, or airbags absorb residual velocity (<3 m/s target).
